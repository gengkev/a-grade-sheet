A Grade Sheet is an online version of those paper gradesheets we get from our teachers. It stores them in your Google Drive, so you can view them anywhere you're signed into it. It imports those progress reports your teachers send biweekly, so you don't have to type a thing. And it even helps you graph your grades, to help you stay on top of them.
